Naïve Bayesian learning for classifying news text articles

Naïve Bayes classifiers are one among the most successful known algorithms for learning to classify text documents. This project classifies text into its appropriate newsgroup. The dataset contains 20,000 newsgroup messages drawn from the 20 newsgroups. The dataset contains 1000 documents from each of the 20 newsgroups.
The 20 news groups:

  • alt.atheism
  • sci.crypt
  • comp.os.ms-windows.misc
  • sci.electronics
  • comp.sys.ibm.pc.hardware
  • sci.med
  • comp.sys.mac.hardware
  • sci.space
  • comp.windows.x
  • soc.religion.christian
  • misc.forsale
  • talk.politics.guns
  • rec.autos
  • talk.politics.mideast
  • rec.motorcycles
  • talk.politics.misc
  • rec.sport.baseball
